Church Digital Twin

Digital model of Església de Santa Maria de l'Alba, Tàrrega, Spain.

Introduction

This project presents a high-resolution 3D model of Església de Santa Maria de l'Alba in Tàrrega, Spain, generated using UAV photogrammetry with a ground sampling distance (GSD) of 4 cm. Aerial imagery was processed in Agisoft Metashape to produce a detailed textured tiled model. The final 3D dataset was published via Cesium ion for interactive web visualization, enabling real-time exploration of the structure and its architectural details in a browser-based environment.
